Milan Map-Athon

Mapping transportation in Milan

Politecnico di Milano is developing a research to map user's movements in various cities with different means of transportation.

Use this application to map your trip in Milan. First, insert your name, keep it consistent and always the same.

Then, select your transportation mode, and finally if you're at the start or end of your trip.

⚠️ Be sure to enable GPS position tracking in your device's settings. ⚠️
